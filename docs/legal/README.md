# Legal

> Legal & Compliance - contracts, compliance, and document management.

The Legal category contains modules for managing legal documents, contracts, and regulatory compliance.

## Modules

| Module | Purpose | Status |
|--------|---------|--------|
| contracts | Contract generation and tracking | Planned |
| compliance | Regulatory compliance tracking | Planned |
| documents | Legal document storage | Planned |

## Key Concepts

### Contract Flow
```
Quote (Sales) → Contract Generated → Sent for Signature → Executed
```

### Compliance Tracking
Track regulatory requirements and deadlines as Open Loops in the Calm Prompt.

## Dependencies

| Depends On | Relationship |

| `sales/quotes` | Quote-to-contract conversion (optional) |
