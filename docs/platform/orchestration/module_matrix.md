# Xentri Module Matrix (The 175)

**Objective:** Define the 175 most important modules, organized by **7 Core Categories** and **35 Elemental Sub-Categories**.

**Theme:** Single-Word Metaphors. Clean, Elemental, Human.

## 1. Bridge (Management & Strategy)

*The Command Center. Connection between vision and reality.*

### 1.1 Soul (Identity)

*The immutable DNA.*

1. **Mission Control:** Editor for Mission, Vision, Purpose, and Origin Story.
2. **Brand DNA:** Voice, Tone, and Personality settings for all Agents.
3. **Values Guard:** An agent that checks decisions against Core Values.
4. **Meeting Copilot:** Listens to strategy meetings, notes, and action items.
5. **Culture Code:** Internal behavioral guidelines and principles.

### 1.2 Pulse (Monitoring)

*The vital signs.*

1. **God View:** The executive dashboard aggregating all Category signals.
2. **KPI Center:** Definition and tracking of North Star and Core Metrics.
3. **Risk Radar:** Anomaly detection agent (Cash runway, Churn spikes).
4. **Sentiment Tracker:** Analysis of Team and Customer mood.
5. **Daily Brief:** Automated morning digest for the CEO.

### 1.3 Horizon (Intelligence)

*The external view.*

1. **Market Watch:** Competitor tracking and news analysis agent.
2. **Trend Spotter:** Industry trend identification and relevance scoring.
3. **SWOT Live:** Dynamic Strength/Weakness/Opp/Threat analysis.
4. **Customer Voice:** Aggregated insights from support/sales conversations.
5. **Scenario Simulator:** "What if" modeling (e.g., "What if market crashes?").

### 1.4 Map (Planning)

*The path forward.*

1. **North Star:** Long-term vision and 10-year target tracker.
2. **OKR Builder:** Objectives & Key Results cascading tool.
3. **Roadmap:** Visual timeline of major initiatives and milestones.
4. **War Room:** Collaborative decision-making space with Agent facilitation.
5. **Decision Log:** Immutable record of key strategic decisions and rationale.

### 1.5 Constitution (Governance)

*The rules of the road.*

1. **Policy Manager:** The central rulebook (Remote work, Expenses, etc.).
2. **Ethics Committee:** AI agent checking for ethical/reputational risks.
3. **Board Deck:** Automated generator for board meeting materials.
4. **Compliance Log:** Audit trail of key strategic decisions.
5. **Access Control:** Governance of who can see/do what (Role definitions).

## 2. Stage (Marketing)

*The Platform. Amplifying the voice.*

### 2.1 Marquee (Presence)

*Being seen.*

1. **Site Builder:** The drag-and-drop website creator (React-based).
2. **Funnel Builder:** High-conversion landing page and A/B test engine.
3. **SEO Agent:** Auto-optimizes meta tags, sitemaps, and keywords.
4. **Local Presence:** Syncs Google Maps, Yelp, and manages local citations.
5. **Domain Guard:** Manages URLs, SSL, and DNS settings.

### 2.2 Studio (Content)

*Creating the story.*

1. **Copywriter:** AI agent that writes blogs, captions, and emails.
2. **Asset Vault:** Central library for images, logos, and videos.
3. **Video Maker:** Simple tool to splice clips and add captions.
4. **Image Gen:** AI art generator for blog posts and ads.
5. **Remix Engine:** Turns 1 blog post into 10 tweets and 1 LinkedIn post.

### 2.3 Broadcast (Distribution)

*Reaching the audience.*

1. **Social Scheduler:** Multi-channel poster (Twitter, LinkedIn, IG).
2. **Ad Manager:** Simple interface for FB/Google Ads.
3. **Email Blaster:** Newsletter and broadcast email tool.
4. **Campaign Orch:** Ties ads, emails, and social into one "Push".
5. **SMS Ping:** Text message marketing tool.

### 2.4 Applause (Reputation)

*Social proof.*

1. **Review Catcher:** Automates requests for Google Reviews after service.
2. **Trust Kit:** Testimonial widgets and verified "Secure" badges.
3. **PR Agent:** Monitors web mentions and suggests responses.
4. **Case Study Builder:** Templates to turn client wins into PDFs.
5. **Influencer Scout:** Finds and vets potential brand ambassadors.

### 2.5 Tribe (Community)

*Deep engagement.*

1. **Event Host:** RSVP and ticket management for webinars/meetups.
2. **Newsletter Pro:** Subscription management and recurring digests.
3. **Member Gate:** Protects content for paying members.
4. **Forum:** Simple discussion board for customers.
5. **Swag Store:** E-commerce lite for branded merch.

## 3. Exchange (Sales)

*The Marketplace. Value for value.*

### 3.1 Magnet (Inbound)

*Capturing interest.*

1. **Capture Kit:** Form builder, surveys, and lead magnets.
2. **Scheduler:** "Book a time" link (Calendly replacement).
3. **Chatbot:** 24/7 site agent to answer FAQs and capture leads.
4. **Visitor ID:** Identifies companies visiting your site (IP lookup).
5. **Lead Scorer:** AI that ranks leads (Hot/Warm/Cold).

### 3.2 Hunt (Outbound)

*Finding opportunities.*

1. **Prospector:** Search tool to find leads by industry/role.
2. **Cold Reach:** Automated email sequences for outreach.
3. **LinkedIn Agent:** Helper for connection requests and DMs.
4. **Data Enricher:** Adds phone/email/company size to raw leads.
5. **List Cleaner:** Verifies email addresses to prevent bounce.

### 3.3 Flow (Pipeline)

*Managing the deal stream.*

1. **Deal Board:** Kanban view of the sales pipeline.
2. **Activity Logger:** Tracks calls, emails, and meetings automatically.
3. **Forecaster:** Predicts revenue based on deal stage and probability.
4. **Territory Map:** Assigns leads to sales reps by region.
5. **Call Coach:** AI analysis of sales calls for coaching insights.

### 3.4 Handshake (Closing)

*Sealing the deal.*

1. **Quote Builder:** Generates price estimates (PDF/Web).
2. **Proposal Gen:** AI that writes the "Why Us" section of proposals.
3. **Contract Sign:** E-signature tool.
4. **Negotiator:** Agent that suggests discount strategies.
5. **Demo Room:** Interactive presentation environment for clients.

### 3.5 Register (Transaction)

*Taking payment.*

1. **POS Terminal:** Interface for in-person card payments.
2. **Checkout Page:** Secure hosted payment page for links.
3. **Subscription Manager:** Handles recurring billing and dunning.
4. **Payment Gateway:** The connection to Stripe/PayPal.
5. **Refund Agent:** Handles dispute resolution and refunds.

## 4. Vault (Finance)

*The Storehouse. Managing resources.*

### 4.1 Inflow (Receivables)

*Money coming in.*

1. **Invoice Gen:** Smart invoice builder with "Pay Now" buttons.
2. **Recurring Bill:** Engine for subscriptions and retainers.
3. **Dunning Agent:** Polite automated chasers for late payments.
4. **Credit Check:** Risk score for new clients before work starts.
5. **Payment Link:** Quick URL generator for ad-hoc charges.

### 4.2 Outflow (Payables)

*Money going out.*

1. **Expense Scan:** OCR tool for receipts and employee reimbursement.
2. **Bill Pay:** Schedule and approve vendor payments (ACH/Wire).
3. **Vendor Portal:** Self-serve area for suppliers to upload invoices.
4. **Approval Flow:** Logic for "If > $500, ask Manager".
5. **Sub Tracker:** Identifies and cancels unused SaaS subscriptions.

### 4.3 Treasury (Cash)

*Liquidity management.*

1. **Bank Sync:** Real-time feed from bank accounts (Plaid/Teller).
2. **Cash Flow:** Visual "River" of money in/out with 30-day forecast.
3. **Capital Agent:** Suggests when to draw on credit lines or invest.
4. **FX Manager:** Multi-currency exchange rate tracker.
5. **Card Control:** Virtual card issuance and limit setting.

### 4.4 Ledger (Accounting)

*The record.*

1. **General Ledger:** Chart of accounts and journal entry interface.
2. **Fixed Assets:** Depreciation tracker for equipment.
3. **Tax Jar:** Automated sales tax calculation and "set aside".
4. **Reconciler:** Tinder-style "Match" interface for bank vs. books.
5. **Audit Trail:** Immutable log of every financial change.

### 4.5 Forecast (Planning)

*The future model.*

1. **Budget Builder:** Department-level budget setting.
2. **Unit Economics:** Calculator for CAC, LTV, and Gross Margin.
3. **Burn Rate:** Runway calculator for startups.
4. **Scenario Model:** "Best Case / Worst Case" financial toggle.
5. **Investor Rep:** Automated PDF generator for monthly updates.

## 5. Engine (Operations)

*The Machine. Execution and delivery.*

### 5.1 Workshop (Service Delivery)

*Doing the work.*

1. **Project Board:** Kanban/List view for client deliverables.
2. **Task Master:** Personal to-do list for every employee.
3. **Time Keeper:** Stopwatch and timesheet logger.
4. **Resource Plan:** "Who is free next week?" visualizer.
5. **SOP Builder:** AI that watches Loom videos and writes Step-by-Step docs.

### 5.2 Supply (Logistics)

*Moving the atoms.*

1. **Inventory Count:** Real-time stock levels and low-stock alerts.
2. **PO Gen:** Purchase Order creator for suppliers.
3. **Supplier DB:** CRM for vendors (contacts, terms, rating).
4. **Ship Label:** Integration with FedEx/UPS for printing labels.
5. **Barcode Scan:** Mobile app interface for warehouse picking.

### 5.3 Habitat (Facilities)

*The space.*

1. **Front Desk:** Visitor log and room booking system.
2. **Fleet Track:** GPS and maintenance tracking for company vehicles.
3. **Asset Tag:** Database of laptops, chairs, and equipment.
4. **Fix It:** Maintenance request ticketing system.
5. **Virtual HQ:** "Gather.town" style link for remote teams.

### 5.4 Concierge (Support)

*Helping the customer.*

1. **Ticket Inbox:** Shared email inbox for <support@company.com>.
2. **Knowledge Base:** Public help center builder.
3. **SLA Tracker:** Countdown timer for "Time to Reply".
4. **CSAT Poll:** "How was our service?" survey sender.
5. **Issue Triage:** AI agent that tags and routes tickets.

### 5.5 Grid (Technology)

*The infrastructure.*

1. **Device Mgmt:** MDM-lite for company laptops.
2. **Software Lic:** Tracker for who has a seat on what tool.
3. **Pass Vault:** Shared password manager for teams.
4. **Access Log:** Who logged into Xentri and when.
5. **Security Scan:** Automated check for weak settings/policies.

## 6. Guild (Team)

*The People. Culture and growth.*

### 6.1 Draft (Recruiting)

*Finding talent.*

1. **Job Board:** Hosted careers page with "Apply Now".
2. **ATS Lite:** Kanban board for applicants (Applied -> Interview -> Offer).
3. **Offer Gen:** Automated offer letter builder with e-sign.
4. **Scorecard:** Interview feedback form for hiring managers.
5. **Headhunter:** Agent that scrapes LinkedIn for candidates.

### 6.2 Welcome (Onboarding)

*Starting right.*

1. **Handbook:** Digital employee manual with "I agree" tracking.
2. **Day 1 Bot:** Automated Slack/Email welcome sequence.
3. **Equip Request:** Form to order laptop/monitor/swag.
4. **Training Track:** Checklist of videos/docs to read in Week 1.
5. **Buddy Match:** Assigns a mentor to new hires.

### 6.3 Admin (Administration)

*The basics.*

1. **Payroll Sync:** Connector to Gusto/ADP (or simple internal calculator).
2. **Time Off:** PTO request and approval workflow.
3. **Org Chart:** Visual tree of who reports to whom.
4. **Doc Vault:** Secure storage for employment contracts/W2s.
5. **Benefits Hub:** Links to health/401k portals.

### 6.4 Growth (Performance)

*Leveling up.*

1. **1:1 Tracker:** Shared agenda for manager/report meetings.
2. **Goal Setter:** Individual goals linked to Company OKRs.
3. **360 Review:** Peer feedback collection tool.
4. **Skill Matrix:** Visual map of team capabilities.
5. **Praise Wall:** Public feed for "High Fives" and recognition.

### 6.5 Spirit (Culture)

*Staying connected.*

1. **Culture Pulse:** Aggregated sentiment, birthdays, and suggestions.
2. **Event Cal:** RSVP for happy hours and offsites.
3. **Alumni Net:** Directory and newsletter for former employees.
4. **Wellness Coach:** AI agent monitoring for burnout signals.
5. **Kudos Bank:** Store for redeeming points/praise for rewards.

## 7. Shield (Legal)

*The Protection. Risk and compliance.*

### 7.1 Entity (Corporate)

*The structure.*

1. **Cap Table:** Simple ledger of who owns what % (Equity).
2. **Board Mins:** Tool to record and sign board meeting minutes.
3. **Incorp Docs:** Vault for Articles of Incorporation/Bylaws.
4. **Stock Cert:** Digital generator for share certificates.
5. **Reg Agent:** Reminder system for state filings.

### 7.2 Pacts (Commercial)

*The agreements.*

1. **MSA Gen:** Master Services Agreement builder.
2. **NDA Sender:** Quick "Sign this NDA" link.
3. **Vendor Log:** Database of all active supplier contracts.
4. **SOW Builder:** Statement of Work generator for projects.
5. **Renewal Alert:** Nudge 30 days before a contract expires.

### 7.3 Moat (IP)

*The assets.*

1. **TM Watch:** Agent that checks if others are using your brand.
2. **Patent Log:** Tracker for invention disclosures.
3. **Copyright:** Registry for creative assets.
4. **Domain Portfolio:** List of all owned web domains.
5. **Brand Protect:** Takedown request generator for copycats.

### 7.4 Code (Compliance)

*The laws.*

1. **Privacy Shield:** GDPR/CCPA requests and Cookie consent manager.
2. **License Track:** Database of professional licenses (e.g., CPA, MD).
3. **Permit Vault:** Storage for city/state business permits.
4. **Access Check:** ADA compliance scanner for the website.
5. **Data Map:** Visualizer of where customer data lives.

### 7.5 Guard (Risk)

*The safety net.*

1. **Insure Policy:** Vault for Liability/E&O policy docs.
2. **COI Request:** Generator for "Certificate of Insurance".
3. **Dispute Log:** Tracker for threatened lawsuits.
4. **Incident Rep:** Form to log workplace accidents/security breaches.
5. **Waiver Gen:** Liability waiver builder for events/services.
