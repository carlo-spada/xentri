# Finance Category Commission

> **Atom ID:** `SYS.003`
> **Type:** commission
> **Status:** Approved

---

## Commission

The Constitution hereby commissions the **Finance Category** to exist as a Strategic Container responsible for financial operations and reporting.

---

## Essential Requirements

The Finance Category MUST:

1. **Manage Invoicing** — Create, send, and track invoices
2. **Process Payments** — Payment collection and reconciliation
3. **Generate Reports** — Financial reporting and analytics
4. **Integrate with Soul** — Financial health reflected in business DNA

---

## Child Commissions

These essentials become commissions for child atoms:

| Essential        | Becomes Child Commission             |
