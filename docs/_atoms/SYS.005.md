# Operations Category Commission

> **Atom ID:** `SYS.005`
> **Type:** commission
> **Status:** Approved

---

## Commission

The Constitution hereby commissions the **Operations Category** to exist as a Strategic Container responsible for business workflows and automation.

---

## Essential Requirements

The Operations Category MUST:

1. **Manage Workflows** — Business process definition and execution
2. **Enable Automation** — Rule-based and AI-driven task automation
3. **Provide Monitoring** — Operational visibility and alerting
4. **Integrate with Soul** — Operations aligned with business DNA
