# Legal Category Commission

> **Atom ID:** `SYS.006`
> **Type:** commission
> **Status:** Approved

---

## Commission

The Constitution hereby commissions the **Legal Category** to exist as a Strategic Container responsible for contracts, compliance, and legal document management.

---

## Essential Requirements

The Legal Category MUST:

1. **Manage Contracts** — Contract creation, negotiation, and storage
2. **Ensure Compliance** — Regulatory compliance tracking and reporting
3. **Handle Documents** — Legal document generation and versioning
4. **Integrate with Soul** — Legal posture aligned with business DNA
